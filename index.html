About ZenPen 2

ZenPen 2 is a modification of Zenpen, a minimalist writing zone by Tim Holman (@twholman). With a clean interface, the focus is on the writing. ZenPen 2 is a modified version written by Hemanth Soni that includes basic interface upgrades. Both ZenPen and ZenPen 2 are open source on Github. If you have any questions, you can email the original developer by clicking here, or get in touch with me by clicking here.

This is ZenPen 2

A minimalist writing zone, where you can block out all distractions and get to what's important: the writing! Now with minor improvements by Hemanth Soni.

To get started, all you need to do is delete this text (just highlight it and hit delete), and start writing. Your work is completely secure - nothing you write here can be seen by anyone other than you. All information is saved on your own computer.

You can use bold, italics, both and urls just by highlighting the text and selecting them from the tiny options box that appears above it.

Quotes are easy to add too!

For questions and open source info, Click that little question mark at the bottom left of the screen.

Happy Writing!
